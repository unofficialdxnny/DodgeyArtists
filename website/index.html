About Artists

Snoop Dogg

Snoop Dogg, whose real name is Calvin Cordozar Broadus Jr.,
is a well-known American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor.
He gained fame in the 1990s and is considered one of the pioneers of West Coast hip-hop.

2PAC

Tupac Shakur, a renowned American rapper and actor. He was born on June 16, 1971,
and tragically passed away on September 13, 1996, at the age of 25. Tupac Shakur
is considered one of the most influential and iconic figures in the history of hip-hop music.

50 Cent

Curtis James Jackson III (born July 6, 1975),
known professionally as 50 Cent, is an American rapper,
actor, television producer, and businessman.
