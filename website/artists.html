About Frenzo Harami

Frenzo Harami is a British rapper and musician known for his contributions to the UK music scene. He gained recognition for his unique style and his controversial lyrics that often touch on gritty and real-life subjects.

Early Life and Background

Frenzo Harami, whose real name is not widely known, was born and raised in the United Kingdom. He had a challenging upbringing and faced various problems in his life.

Music Career

Frenzo Harami began taking his music career seriously around 2018. He is known for his rap music and has released several songs and albums throughout his career.

Notable Songs

Frenzo Harami gained prominence with his single "Chaabian Boyz," which he collaborated on with Shaker the Baker. This song received attention but also faced controversy due to its explicit content, including lyrics that referenced a prostitution ring.

Album Releases

In the same year, Frenzo released his debut 5-track extended play (EP) album titled "Gundeh Paseh." The EP received attention and helped establish Frenzo in the music industry.

Chaabian Boyz Volume 2

Frenzo Harami released "Chaabian Boyz: Volume 2" in January 2020. The album featured vocals from Shaker the Baker and quickly gained popularity, with over 500,000 streams within 24 hours of its release.
